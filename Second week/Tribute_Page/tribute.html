Bill Gate

America business magnate




Bill Gate, with family name, William Henry Gates III was born on October 28,1955. He is a software developer, investor, author, and philanthropist. He is a co-founder of Microsoft, along with his late childhood friend Paul Allen. During his career at Microsoft, Gates held the positions of chairman, chief executive officer (CEO), president and chief software architect, while also being the largest individual shareholder until May 2014. He was a major entrepreneur of the microcomputer revolution of the 1970s and 1980s.

Timeline

  • In 1963, He co-developed the programming language, BASIC, for Altair.

  • Gates is among the pioneers of the personal computer revolution.
  • He impacted the way the world works; more than 4 decades after, more than a billion PCs are in use today.
  • In 2016, he won the presidential medal of freedom.
